# Meerkat Brand Identity

## Name

**Meerkat** — always capitalised as a proper noun when referring to the project. Lowercase `meerkat` in code contexts (package names, CLI commands, metadata).

## Concept

Meerkats are sentinels. They stand upright, scan the horizon for threats and opportunities, and relay intelligence back to the group. The mascot embodies what the framework does: vigilant scanning of the competitive landscape, alert pattern recognition, and strategic awareness — always on the lookout so PMs can act with confidence.

## Logo Variants

| File | Use case |
|------|----------|
| `logo.svg` | Full mascot — standing meerkat in circle badge. Hero images, landing pages, social cards. |
| `logo-mark.svg` | Head-only mark — compact circle. Favicons, app icons, avatars, small spaces. |
| `logo-wordmark.svg` | Head mark + "meerkat" text. README headers, documentation, slide decks. |

## Typography

The wordmark uses the system sans-serif stack (`system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif`) at **bold (700) weight** with tight letter-spacing (-1). This ensures consistent rendering without requiring a custom font.

For documentation and UI contexts:
- **Headings:** Bold weight, Midnight colour
- **Body:** Regular weight, Midnight or Slate
- **Code:** Monospace, same colour scheme

## Tone of Voice

Meerkat's personality mirrors the animal:

- **Alert, not anxious** — identifies risks clearly without catastrophising
- **Direct, not blunt** — honest assessments with practical next steps
- **Curious, not naive** — asks sharp questions, doesn't accept surface answers
- **Collaborative, not passive** — a thinking partner, not a yes-machine

## Usage Guidelines

- Always maintain clear space around the logo mark (minimum: 25% of the mark's diameter on each side)
- On dark backgrounds, the circle badge works as-is (the Midnight circle provides its own framing)
- On light backgrounds, use the logo without the background circle, or ensure sufficient contrast
- Don't stretch, rotate, or recolour the mascot
- The head-only mark (`logo-mark.svg`) is preferred at sizes below 48px where the full body loses detail
